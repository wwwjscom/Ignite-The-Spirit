Welcome

The Ignite the Spirit Fund was created to raise funds for Chicago firefighters and their families in times of need. By supporting the Ignite the Spirit Fund, you’ll be helping the firefighters take care of their own. It’s your chance to give something back to them for doing so much for the communities in which we live. [Read more]

January | James McGann

Chicago Firefighter/Paramedic James McGann says he gets the same rush from jumping out of an airplane that he does running into a burning building to try to save someone. “There has always been an interest in this field for me because while it looks very exciting you can still help people in their time of need.” [Read More]

August 13, 2008
We are planning a cancer awareness bike ride that will take us around Lake Micigan in early spring of 2009. The ride will be a 12 day 900 mile ride that will take us up through Micigan and back down through Wisconsin. This ride will be Ignite the Spirit's way of helping with the fight against child cancer.
