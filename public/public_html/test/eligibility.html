$5,000 High School Scholarship Eligibility

2008 - 2009 School Year

Who is Eligible

Any student during the 2008 – 2009 school year entering or presently in High School and plan on attending a tuition paying High School. The applicant’s parent or legal guardian must be a Firefighter/Paramedic on the CFD active, retired or deceased. This is a one time scholarship for the one individual who wins.

Selection

All applications will be reviewed by an Ignite the Spirit Scholarship Committee.

Deadline

March 1st, 2008 (1/B)(EMS 3)

Send a Complete Application (on the reverse side) and essay to: Ignite the Spirit Committee Attention: Scholarship Application Committee 5447 West Cullom Chicago, Illinois 60641

Suggestions

Applications will be rated for appearance, grammar, spelling and punctuation. The essay will look best if typed, however handwritten is acceptable. Appearance and grammar are important.

Topic

Write a 100 to 200 word essay on:
“What it Means to be a Member of a Chicago Fire Department Family”.

Recipients Notification

The individual receiving the one $5,000.00 scholarship will be notified by March 14th, 2008. Due to the volume of applications, the recipient will be the ONLY one notified. The winner receiving the scholarship will be posted on the Ignite the Spirit website after the winner is notified.

Good Luck to all the applicants. We are looking forward to Reading everyone’s entries!!
